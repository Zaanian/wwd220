White Space and Clean Design

www.churchofjesuschrist.org

Organization: Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

ChurchofJesusChrist.org has a very clean look to it. There is proper spacing so that there is a lot of content but it does not feel cluttered or disorganized, nor do I feel overwhelmed when looking through the content.

Hick's Law

www.games-workshop.com

Company: Games Workshop

games-workshop.com is a sight to order gaming models. The website applys Hick's law by using images, filters, and stylized logo's. it easier to select what you want with filters to sort the products and every product has an image that shows you exactly what you will be getting.

Rule of Thirds

www.forgeworld.co.uk

Company: Games Workshop

Forgeworld follows the Rule of Thirds principle with all of it's images. It is a site that sells models and the best way to do that is to show the model.
