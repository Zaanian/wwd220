Audience

People looking for job oppurturnities or are wanting to start a business. Those trying to find certain businesses or seeing if a certain type of business is in the area.

Persona

John williams, 25, Has just graduated College and has moved back home (Yelm).

Brandon Smith, 35, Already has a business (something). Is not familiar with the area.

Scenarios

1. John Williams moved back home after going to college. He needs to know what businesses are in the area that he can apply the skills he has just learned.

2. Brandon Smith has a business. He is looking to expand his brand into other areas. The website can help inform him if this is the right area to expand to.

3. Brandon Smith is new to the area and would like to know what recreational activities are in the area.

4. John Williams meets some friends and they want to start a restaurant. They need food supplies. The site can help them get in contact with a business that sells fresh food.

5. Brandon has a son that is ready to be enrolled in school. He would ike to know what schooling options are available.
